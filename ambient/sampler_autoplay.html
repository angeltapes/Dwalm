Keyboard sampler

Start and stop

  • A - record
  • S - start
  • D - stop
  • Q to E - change pitch

Loop length and tempo

  • Z to B - loop length: quick change
  • N - decrease loop length
  • M - increase loop length
  • P - increase tempo
  • L - decrease tempo

Reverb and delay

  • Y - reset
  • R - increase reverb time
  • F - decrease reverb time
  • T - increase reverb decay rate
  • G - decrease reverb rate
  • U - increase delay time
  • J - decrease delay time
  • I - increase delay feedback
  • K - decrease delay feedback
