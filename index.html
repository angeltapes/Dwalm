dwalm

P5 sound and graphics experiments. These were built on a PC laptop. Best of luck getting them to work on your device. :)

random sampler - your computer randomly samples and loops ambient sound

frequency sampler - play samples based on the audio input frequencies

oscar - mouse-operated synth. Controls:

  • left click - play
  • middle wheel - play and adjust the starting frequencies
  • right click - change the voice
  • mouse wheel up/down - play crazy bends

sampler autoplay - keyboard operated looping machine. This needs a browser where you can enable the mic (not Safari, sorry).

oscar da mouse - mouse click and drag to create oscillators and trails. Press 1-4 to pick a constantly-playing oscillator. Click the mouse again to stop the oscillator.

prepared piano - connect a piano to the audio-in socket of a soundcard and start oscillators and triggered samples.

click n play - click and drag a mouse to create a looping oscillator.

keyboard oscillator - use your computer keyboard to play scales.
